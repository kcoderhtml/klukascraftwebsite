    OUR HISTORY

    KlukasCraft has been around for over 6 months now. What started off as a small laptop based minecraft server with only one survival world has developed into a Multiverse with Creative and Survival modes, a comprehensive GriefProtection system to keep people's creations safe, a WebMap, and this website.

    OUR STAFF

    The KlukasCraft staff team are friendly, dedicated, and always monitoring the server to help everything run smoothly. Have any questions? Make sure to Contact Us to reach out!

    OUR SERVERS

    We have Creative, Globe Survival, and Terra Survival on our network, all with fully custom builds and useful perks and features. Join today at mc.sytes.net!

    OUR GAMES

    Creative

    Creative is a gamemode where you start in a forest land with WorldEdit and Creative. Compete with others to build amazing bulds or join them and have fun! Fun challenges are awaiting for you through your Creative adventure! It takes Fun, creativity, and a bit of building to build your amazing build! So Come Play Now!

    Globe Survival

    Globe Survival was our first world as a server. It is a survival gamemode where you claim land, build a base with your friends, and attempt to find other bases. We have several awesome features such as Pueblo Claims and a handy hacks allowed rule. Compete with other players and strive to become the top base/player, today!

    Terra Survival

    Terra Survival is a beatiful, survial world packed with little touches! Chose your land to claim and join the players to build amazing things and have fun. Explore our custom map and test your skills on survival in giant structures. Whether you are an pacifist, explorer or adventurer there is a spot for you. Join the exploration today on KlukasCraft!
